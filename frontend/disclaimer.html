Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: June 2026

⚠️ Important Notice

Diabetes Control AI is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

1. AI-Generated Content

All recommendations, analyses, meal suggestions, risk assessments, and health plans generated by this application are produced by artificial intelligence (Amazon Bedrock, Claude 3 Haiku). While designed to be helpful and evidence-informed, AI outputs may contain errors, omissions, or inaccuracies.

2. Not a Doctor

The AI Coach and analysis tools do NOT replace your physician, endocrinologist, dietitian, or any qualified healthcare professional. Always:

  • Consult your doctor before changing medications or dosages
  • Seek immediate medical attention for emergencies
  • Verify AI-generated lab interpretations with your healthcare team
  • Follow your prescribed treatment plan

3. Emergency Situations

If you experience any of the following, call 999 (Malaysia) or go to the nearest emergency department immediately:

  • Blood glucose above 300 mg/dL with nausea, vomiting, or confusion
  • Blood glucose below 54 mg/dL and unable to self-treat
  • Signs of DKA: fruity breath, rapid breathing, severe abdominal pain
  • Chest pain, difficulty breathing, sudden numbness
  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide — call 988 (Befrienders Malaysia: 03-7956 8145)

4. Accuracy Limitations

The AI may:

  • Misread values from lab photos (especially blurry or low-quality images)
  • Provide general guidance that doesn't account for your specific comorbidities
  • Not have access to your complete medical history
  • Generate responses based on general clinical guidelines that may not apply to your specific case

5. Doctor Interactions

Doctors on this platform provide asynchronous guidance based on data you share. This does NOT constitute a formal doctor-patient relationship, clinical consultation, or official medical record. For formal consultations, visit your healthcare provider in person.

6. Your Responsibility

By using this Service, you acknowledge that:

  • You are responsible for your own health decisions
  • AI recommendations are informational, not prescriptive
  • You will not hold the Service liable for health outcomes
  • You will seek professional medical advice for any health concerns

7. Clinical Evidence

Our AI system prompt references the American Diabetes Association (ADA) Standards of Care 2024. However, guidelines change, and individual circumstances vary. Your healthcare team's advice takes precedence over any AI recommendation.
